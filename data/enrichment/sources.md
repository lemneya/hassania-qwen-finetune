## Books and Literature Sources

### Poetry Collections
1. **ديوان ولد انجرتو** (Archive.org)
   - Hassani folk poetry collection
   - URL: https://archive.org/details/akforda_yahoo

2. **جماليات الشعر العربي الحساني** (PDF)
   - Aesthetics of Hassani Arabic Poetry
   - URL: https://sadanatoualharf.com/sites/default/files/files/جماليات%20الشعر%20العربي%20الحساني.pdf

3. **كتاب امروك الحرف** (PDF)
   - First composition in Hassaniya dialect (19th century)
   - Historical Hassaniya text

### Academic Resources
1. **Hassaniya Arabic (Mali): Poetic and Ethnographic Texts** (Scribd)
   - URL: https://www.scribd.com/doc/205106261/Hassaniya-Arabic-Mali-Poetic-and-Ethnographic-Texts

2. **Basic English-Hassaniya Dictionary** (Archive.org)
   - 8000+ words, phrases, sentences
   - URL: https://archive.org/details/eng-hass-dict-a-z

3. **Western Sahara Poetry Collection** (Five Colleges)
   - Hassaniya Arabic & English translations
   - URL: https://www.langmedia.fivecolleges.edu/resources/western-sahara/arts-entertainment/poetry-and-song

### Linguistic Studies
1. **Ḥassāniyya Arabic** by C. Taine-Cheikh (2007)
   - Academic paper on Hassaniya linguistics
   - URL: https://shs.hal.science/halshs-00563853/file/CTC_EALL_Hassaniyya.pdf
